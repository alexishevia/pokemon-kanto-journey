# My Journey Through Kanto

Document your Pokemon FireRed/LeafGreen journey through Kanto using a nice visual timeline of your team at each gym battle.

**Live site:** https://pokemon-kanto-journey.alexishevia.com/

## Features

- Select your team at each gym leader milestone
- Auto-generated canvas image showing your journey progression
- Pokemon grouped by evolution family — see your team evolve and track when Pokemon were swapped
- Reorder milestones to match your actual playthrough
- Searchable dropdowns with sprite previews
- Download your journey as a PNG image

## How to Use

1. Open the app in your browser
2. For each gym leader, select the Pokemon you had on your team
3. Use the arrow buttons to reorder milestones if needed
4. Right-click the preview image to save, or click "Download as PNG"
